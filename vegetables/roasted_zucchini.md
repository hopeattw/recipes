# Roasted Zucchini

# Prep Time 
5 mins prep
5 mins cook
( didn't factor in oven preheating )

# Ingredients
- zucchini
- s&p 
- olive oil

# Instructions
1. Preheat to 450 F
2. Wash and cut zucchini into rounds 
3. Toss zucchini in olive oil, salt and pepper
4. Line and place zucchini on trays making sure they don't overlap
5. Roast for 5 mins
