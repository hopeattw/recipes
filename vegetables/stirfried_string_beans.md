# Stir-fried String Beans

## Prep time
20-30 mins

## Ingredients
- string beans
- garlic
- spicy pickled radish

## Instructions
1. Bring pot of water to boil
2. Clean string beans
3. Boil string beans until just done (will be stir frying later)
4. Either take the string beans out or push them to the side and add some oil
5. Add garlic and fry until fragrant 
6. Add spicy pickled radish until warmed through and oils are out
7. Toss string beans with the oil and add salt to taste
