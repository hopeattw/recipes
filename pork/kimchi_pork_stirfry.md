# Kimchi Pork Stirfry

## Prep Time 
5 mins prep
10 mins cook

## Ingredients
+ sliced pork
+ kimchi
+ soy sauce
+ sesame oil

## Instructions
1. Heat sesame oil on medium-high
2. Slice kimchi to bite sized pieces
3. Fry kimchi until warmed through
4. Add sliced pork 
5. When close to done, add some soy sauce to taste
